What you will learn in DAAA

Year 1 focuses on building computing fundamentals such as programming and learning computer parts. The first semester in Year 1 is the same for all School of Computing courses, but become more specialised toward the second semester.

Year 2 integrates everything to Artificial Intelligence and the things you can do with it.

Year 3 is mostly spent on your Final Year Project (FYP) and in your internship. Thus, the content taught in Year 3 will be very minimal.
